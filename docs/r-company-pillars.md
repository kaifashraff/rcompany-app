# R Company Pillars

Derived from Kaif's 2026-05-27 voice note.

## Transcript Gist

R Company should not be a generic order website. It should be the app Kaif can show to any market shop, boutique owner, wholesaler, menswear seller, or fabric trader and say: place your production work here.

The buyer may need only hand zari/embroidery, only stitching by a master, fabric sourcing, color/dyeing coordination, urgent capacity, or full finished-garment outsourcing. R Company should stay centered on artisan zari embroidery while expanding into the surrounding production network.

## Pillars

1. B2B market acquisition
   R Company is sold directly to shops, boutiques, and wholesalers as their production backend.

2. Artisan zari core
   The main wedge is specialist hand zari and embroidery work through trusted karigars.

3. Flexible outsourcing
   Buyers can choose embroidery-only, master-stitching-only, fabric/dyeing support, rush capacity, or full garment outsourcing.

4. Master and karigar routing
   Every order becomes a production packet routed between zari karigar, master tailor, fabric source, dyer, QC, and dispatch.

5. White-label delivery layer
   R Company can return work to the shopkeeper or deliver finished pieces directly to the end customer while the boutique owns the relationship.
